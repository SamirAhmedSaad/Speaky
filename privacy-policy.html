1. Introduction

Welcome to Speaky AI, an AI-powered English learning app for Android. Speaky helps you improve your English through interactive voice conversations, text-based chat with an AI tutor, curated reading articles, a daily vocabulary feature, and a community space — all designed to make language learning natural, effective, and engaging.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Speaky AI app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices. By using Speaky AI, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide and improve the app experience:

  • Voice Input: When you use speaking or pronunciation features, the app accesses your microphone. Voice data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous data about how you interact with the app — features used, session duration, learning progress — may be collected to improve the experience.
  • Device Information: Basic technical information such as device model, OS version, and app version may be collected for diagnostics and analytics.
  • Account Information: If you sign in to use the Community feature, we collect your display name, profile photo (if provided), and the email address associated with your Google account via Firebase Authentication.
  • Community Content: Messages you send in the Community chat are stored on our Firebase Firestore servers so other users can receive them. This content is associated with your account.
  • Advertising Data: Our third-party ad partner (Google AdMob) may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant advertisements. See the Advertising section for details.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • App Functionality: To provide core features — AI tutoring, voice practice, articles, daily word, and community chat.
  • AI Processing: To power personalized feedback and conversation through the on-device AI engine.
  • Personalization: To remember your learning preferences, progress, and settings.
  • Analytics: To understand how the app is used and identify areas for improvement.
  • Advertising: To display ads through Google AdMob that help keep the app free.
  • Community: To enable real-time messaging between users in the community feature.
  • Crash Reporting: To identify and fix technical issues using Firebase Crashlytics.

4. On-Device AI Processing

Privacy by Design

Your voice stays on your device. Speaky's AI engine runs entirely on-device. When you speak or practice pronunciation, your voice audio is processed locally — it is never recorded, uploaded, or sent to our servers or any external service.

Text-based interactions with the AI tutor may be processed to generate responses, but are not stored beyond your current session unless you explicitly save them within the app.

5. User Accounts & Community

The Community feature requires you to sign in with a Google account via Firebase Authentication. When you sign in:

  • Your Google display name and profile photo are stored in Firebase Firestore to show your identity to other community members.
  • Messages you send in community chats are stored on Firebase Firestore servers and visible to other users in that chat.
  • You can delete your account and associated community data by contacting us at privacy@speaky.app.

Users who do not use the Community feature are not required to create an account, and no account data is collected for them.

6. Third-Party Services

Speaky AI uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — advertising. Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Analytics — anonymous usage statistics. Firebase Privacy
  • Firebase Crashlytics — crash and error reporting. Firebase Privacy
  • Firebase Authentication — sign-in for community features. Firebase Privacy
  • Firebase Firestore — community chat storage. Firebase Privacy

We do not sell your personal data to any third party. Data shared with these services is limited to what is necessary for their stated purpose.

7. Advertising & Consent

We use Google AdMob to display banner advertisements within the app. AdMob may collect:

  • Android Advertising ID (AAID)
  • IP address
  • Device usage data

This data is used to serve personalized or non-personalized ads depending on your consent.

EU / EEA Users: On first launch, you will be shown a consent dialog asking whether you agree to personalized ads. If you decline, ads will still be shown but will not be personalized. Your choice is stored and respected. You may update your preference at any time through your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

All users can opt out of personalized advertising at any time through Android device settings.

All ad content is restricted to a General (G) rating — no adult, violent, or inappropriate ad content is served.

8. Data Retention

  • Local Storage: All learning data (vocabulary progress, conversation history, settings) is stored on your device only and deleted when you uninstall the app or clear app data.
  • Community Data: Messages and profile data stored in Firebase Firestore are retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion by contacting privacy@speaky.app.
  • Analytics & Crash Reports: Retained according to Google Firebase's data retention policies.
  • Advertising Data: Retained according to Google AdMob's data retention policies.

9. Children's Privacy

Speaky AI is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used the app and provided personal information, please contact us at privacy@speaky.app and we will take immediate steps to remove any associated data.

10. App Permissions

Speaky AI requests the following Android permissions, used solely for the purposes described:

  • RECORD_AUDIO: Required for voice speaking and pronunciation practice. Audio is processed on-device and never transmitted externally.
  • INTERNET: Required to load articles, serve ads via AdMob, sync community messages, and use AI features.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Used to send optional daily word reminders. You can disable notifications at any time from device settings.

11. Your Rights (GDPR — EU / EEA Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You may request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to Erasure: You may request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to Restriction: You may request that we restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability: You may request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: You may object to processing of your personal data for direct marketing or based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You may withdraw consent for personalized advertising at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

The data controller for personal data processed under this policy is: Samir Hayan, reachable at privacy@speaky.app.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@speaky.app. We will respond within 30 days.

12. Your Rights (All Users)

  • Opt out of personalized advertising via Android device settings.
  • Delete all local app data by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.
  • Request deletion of your community account and associated data by emailing privacy@speaky.app.
